FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I download the application?

A: You can download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store.

Q: How do I add cards for businesses?

A: You can add cards by navigating to the "Add Cards" section in the app and following the instructions.

Q: How do I collect stamps?

A: Simply show your card in the store on your phone, and the staff will add stamps to your card.

Q: What rewards can I get?

A: The rewards vary by business, but they can include discounts, free items, or special offers.

Q: Is the app free to use?

A: Yes, the app is completely free to download and use.

Q: How can I contact support?

A: You can reach our support team through the "Contact Us" section in the app.
